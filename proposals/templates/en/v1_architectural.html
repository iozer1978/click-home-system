01 · Who we are

Revolutionising Israeli housing — one delivered unit at a time.

Click Home specializes in the import and distribution of container homes and prefabricated light-construction houses, offering innovative, modern and eco-friendly housing solutions to the Israeli market.

We collaborate with leading manufacturers and are committed to high-quality international certifications, tailored customization, and sustainability across the supply chain.
