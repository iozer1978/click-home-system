01 · Who we are
公司简介

Building your dream — affordable & sustainable.

Click Home specializes in the import and distribution of container homes and prefabricated light-construction houses, offering innovative, modern and eco-friendly housing solutions tailored to the Israeli market.

We collaborate with leading manufacturers — prioritising international certifications, customization, sustainability, and expert installation support from initial consultation to delivery.
